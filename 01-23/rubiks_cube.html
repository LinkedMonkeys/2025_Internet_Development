THE RUBIK'S CUBE

The Rubik's Cube is one of the most fascinating physical puzzles ever made. While simple in it's design, they are tough to solve!

Three Great Things About the Rubik's Cube:

  • Speed-solving a Rubik's Cube is a fun goal to strive towards, and you can even participate in competitions!
  • The Rubik's Cube comes in a variety of shapes and sizes:
    • The 2x2 Rubik's Cube is perfect for introducing puzzles to kids!
    • The 3x3 Rubik's Cube is a classic, and has a lot of depth.
    • The largest Rubik's Cube is 49x49x49, which has 13,839 pieces and weighs about 65 pounds!
  • Novel Rubik's Cube designs make for great desk-toppers!
